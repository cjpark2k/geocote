Evolving Trends in the Metal Coatings Market

Traditional metal protection methods face increasing scrutiny due to environmental impact and performance limitations. The industry is rapidly shifting towards advanced solutions like zinc flake coatings, with a strong emphasis on eco-friendly alternatives.

Traditional Methods

Galvanizing and conventional plating often involve hazardous materials and high energy consumption.

Technological Shift

Zinc flake coatings offer superior corrosion resistance with thinner films and no risk of hydrogen embrittlement.

Market Demand

There is a growing demand for coatings that are both high-performing and environmentally responsible.

The Environmental Imperative: Why Water-Based?

Solvent-based coatings are a major source of Volatile Organic Compound (VOC) emissions, posing risks to health and the environment. Stricter regulations worldwide are accelerating the adoption of water-based alternatives.

VOC Content: A Stark Contrast

GEOCOTE™ water-based solutions dramatically reduce harmful VOCs compared to traditional solvent-based coatings and are on par with leading water-based competitors.
